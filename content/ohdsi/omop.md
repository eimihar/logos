> **OMOP** (Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership) is a common data model and research framework developed by the Observational Health Data Sciences and Informatics (OHDSI) to standardize and analyze real-world healthcare data such as electronic health records, insurance claims, and registries. It defines a unified database structure, controlled vocabularies, and analytical tools so that data from different hospitals, countries, or systems can be transformed into the same format, making large-scale observational studies, drug safety monitoring, and population health research easier, reproducible, and comparable across institutions.
