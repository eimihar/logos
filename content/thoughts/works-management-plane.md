i found a new project management tool called plane, an alternative to asana and notion (what we currently use) on tracking issues and managing tasks

what caught my attention was the intake feature particularly, where we can do triaging around issues/feature requests created by our teams, or even can take request externally through some public link. which is something i've been looking into back then. i used trello then asana personally back then for managing tasks and even details for qmed, i always wished we could have better centralized way of managing our own works even if we work independently. i know that lot of things are due to the pace of new projects, and no amount of management tool can solve this. always better than nothing tbh. 

asana getting pricier, i don't think i'd introduce it to the team. no one really use asana other than me and arun back then. plus, you can self-host this so why not.
